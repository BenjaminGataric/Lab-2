Easy Oven Cooked Pulled Pork

Puleld Pork Recipe by J. Kenji López-alt

Unlike many pulled pork reciepes are useally made in slow cookers. This recipe cooks it in a dutch oven allows even heat distribution which enhances flavour.

Ingredients

  • 5 Tablespoons dark brown sugar
  • 1 tablespoon kosher salt
  • 1 tablespoon paprika
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1 teaspoon freshly ground black pepper
  • 1 teaspoon ground coriander
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground fennel seed
  • Large pinch cayenne pepper
  • 1 whole bone-in or boenless pork butt (5 to 7 pounds, 2.25 to 3kg)
  • 1 cup ketchup
  • 1/2 cup dark molasses
  • 1/2 cup cider vinegar, divided
  • 2 tablespoons Worcestershire sauce
  • 1 tablespoon liquid smoke, such as wright's
  • 1 tablespoon brown mustard
  • 2 teaspoons hot sauce
  • 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
  • 1 large minced onion
  • 1 cup bourbon
  • 1/2 cup chicken stock

Instructions

  1. Place oven rack to the lower position and preheat to 300°F. Combine sugar, salt, paprika, cumin, black pepper, coriander, ground fennel seed, and cayenne pepper in a bowl mix well. Season pork with 2-3 tablespoons spice mixture
  2. Whisk ketchup, molasses, half of cider vinegar, Worcestershire, liquid smoke, mustard and hot sauce in a bowl. Whisk in remaning spice mixture
  3. Heat oil over medium-high heat till shimmering. Add pork and cook, turning occasionally untill well browned on all sides. 5 minutes total. add onion and cook, stirring and scraping browned bits from the bottom of the pan, untill softened in about 2 mins
